About

I’m a 28 year old digital product designer and creative developer. My interests lie in design in general, mobile computing, electronic vehicles and making coffee. I live in Reykjavík, Iceland, with my wonderful fiancé and two kids.

Projects

I make ideas and photo­graphs for myself and user interfaces with a focus on user experience, sim­pli­city and responsive­ness for others. Recent work includes Veldu Rafbíl, the TM app and advisory website, Takumi, QuizUp, Blær and Hvað er í bíó.
