About me

I’m a 29 year old digital product designer and creative developer. My interests lie in design in general, mobile computing, electronic vehicles and making coffee. I live in Reykjavík, Iceland, with my wonderful fiancé and two kids.

I ideate, design and deliver websites and apps using the latest web technology. I might not be immediately available for hire but I love talking about an exciting project or collaboration. Let me know what you are up to: hugihlynsson@gmail.com
